Create A Session

Welcome to the session screen, here you can create a session. Once created they will see the deposit request in their email, and you'll see their deposit info on the document section of the screen. Also on the session section will have information regarding the session. The different areas of this form can be completed in any order. Once complete the session is tentatively set up and waiting for deposit confirmation in order to be ready to go.

Deposit Waiver

A session is only considered made if a deposit is truly secure, and made 24hrs after booking. The payee who fails to make a secure payment will lose their appointment, and be put in a "pay upfront" mode for one week. Up front payment in exchange of a deposit for future session
